Choosing Your Platform

PC-Offload is enabled by mature platforms. The right choice depends on your existing infrastructure and strategic goals.

Key Implementation Decision: Resource Pooling

Dedicated Single-Session VMs

Each user gets their own VM. This offers the best performance and security isolation but comes at a higher cost.

Ideal for: High-performance CAD, sensitive data processing.

Shared Multi-Session VMs

Multiple users share a single, powerful VM. This is highly cost-effective but performance can be variable.

Ideal for: General-purpose applications, large user bases.
